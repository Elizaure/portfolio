Professional Thesis

My thesis searchs to understand how to enhance empathy between professionals and their users by a-day-in-a-life-of method. Alike this IDEO designer, Kristian Simsarian which admitted imself as 'a patient', this experience should provoke a big change in the way of thinking users of professionnals.

However, there is no strong study of which conditions are required and enough to make append this new state of mind.So, by my study, I aim to identify the conditions which provoke this change in the state of mind of decision makers.

Work Psychologist

As a professional, I intervened on differents missions which needed to identify the problems, the needs and make some recommendation to improve the subject's situation.

Therefore in Orange and in GCS, I received some professionals in face-to-face interview and some services in groups interviews. I intervened with them to find out their actual problems, identify the sources of their difficulties and made some recommendations.
