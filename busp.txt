		<h1>Business Projects</h1>
			<h2>Design Thinking Process</h2>
	<p>The process uses in all our Business Projects is the Design Thinking. With the differents steps, we can create products and services adapted to specific users.</p>
			<h2>Eurovia</h2>
	<p>Eurovia is a affiliated company of Vinci Group. They have a specific problematic and with the design thinking process, we aim to understand their users and respond to their needs by innovatives solutions.</p>
	<p>First, we searched to understand the problematic. After, we questioned the users to find out their needs and problems. We presented  our findings to the group project and we identified some working axes. Now we are gathering our knowledge to delve into our innovative solutions and prototyping them.</p>
			<h2>Crédit Mutuel Anjou</h2>
	<p>The Credit Mutuel regional headquarters are on a historic building in Angers. The directors want to adapt the premises to their collaborators and their clients.</p>
	<p>We assess the needs of the clients, collaborators and people which lived in Angers by immerse ourselves in their daily lives. After, we proposed to the project group our findings about needs.</p>
			<h2>Hackhaton City Lab</h2>
	<p>In november and december 2017, we participated to an hackathon organised by L'Alliance. I worked with my team on the urban logistic under the aegis of Niji and Keran Group. We have designed MyHUB : urban hub spread in all city districts. We won the hackathon with this solution.</p>	
				<h2>IMA Protect</h2>
	<p>IMA Protect is a company of protection and surveillance. They want us to find innovative way of use some pictures to immerse their clients and show how their solutions can protect a house.</p>
